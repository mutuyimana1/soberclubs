Sober club: Fostering Collective Transformation

Guidance and Inspiration

Members with religious backgrounds, including pastors and spiritual guides and Individuals who have personally witnessed and supported the transformative journey of others.

Support and Encouragement

Dedicated supporters who actively encourage and foster a positive and sober lifestyle. and Local leaders actively engaged in promoting a healthier and more sober community.

Educational Advocacy

Teachers, academics, and students actively involved in advocating for a healthier lifestyle.

Media Amplification

Journalists who spread awareness, destigmatize sobriety, and share stories of transformation.

Community Well-being and Financial Support

Owners and leaders of NGOs focusing on health and community well-being. and Medical practitioners, healthcare experts, business owners, and corporate allies contributing to overall well-being and financial support..

In unity, Sober Club's diverse team members contribute to these categories, fostering positive transformations and embracing a lifestyle of sobriety and well-being. Sober Club, with its inclusive spirit, welcomes individuals from various backgrounds and professions. It's a dynamic community where diverse stories converge, united by a common goal of promoting a healthier lifestyle and embracing sobriety.

About SOBER CLUB

The strength of the Sober Club lies in its diverse membership, each with a unique story of triumph over alcohol. Among the members are two pastors who, at the age of 13, baptized the Founder of Sober Club Dr. Gamariel Mbonimana and helped embraced a born-again lifestyle.
