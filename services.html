Local and international celebrities from around the world

These individuals not only act as Fans, Friends, Sponsors, and Partners for Social Sober Club events but also serve as inspiring figures who support soberness and sobriety as a healthier way of living . Their participation highlights the global impact of "Keeping Sober" and reinforces the message of strength, resilience, and a thriving sober lifestyle. So far Sober Club has friends and partners in East Africa, South Africa, West Africa, North Africa, Europe, Middle East, Asia, USA and Canada. These members assist Sober Club to achieve activities related to Fostering Collective Transformation, Guidance and Inspiration, Support and Encouragement, Educational Advocacy, Media Amplification, Community Well-being and Financial Support.

The strength of the Sober Club lies in its diverse membership, each with a unique story of triumph over alcohol. Among the members are two pastors who, at the age of 13, baptized the Founder of Sober Club Dr. Gamariel Mbonimana and helped embraced a born-again lifestyle.
