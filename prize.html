INTERNATIONAL ACADEMIC SOBER PRIZE

The International Academic Sober Prize, an innovative initiative by the Sober Club, aims to commend and celebrate university and higher learning institutions for their exceptional efforts in promoting a sober lifestyle and community engagement. This global groundbreaking prize is positioned to inspire positive change and resilience among current university students

Read More

Background

In response to a critical concern revealed by a June 2022 survey from the Rwanda Biomedical Centre, indicating a worrisome increase in alcohol consumption and illegal drug use among Rwandan adolescents, the Sober Club, a dedicated social enterprise committed to community well-being, is launching the Academic Sober Prize. This initiative strives to create a supportive community for individuals committed to a substance-free lifestyle.

Read More

Objective of the Academic Sober Prize

The primary goal is to acknowledge and promote university and higher learning institution-led initiatives that cultivate a culture of sobriety and community development. By recognizing outstanding projects in various categories, the prize aspires to inspire healthier and more responsible lifestyles among the student community

Read More

Eligibility Criteria

The International Academic Sober Prize welcomes all admitted university students with startup and existing projects that promote sobriety and community development. This inclusive criterion ensures diverse participation and encourages a broad range of innovative solutions.

Read More

Selection Process

A rigorous and transparent process is integral to the International Academic Sober Prize. A panel comprising university lecturers and deans of students from participating campuses will meticulously evaluate project submissions. Criteria include innovation, impact, scalability, and sustainability. Five students from each participating campus will be selected for the International level, where interviews will determine the ultimate winner in each category.

Read More

Prizes and Recognition

Recipients of the International Academic Sober Prize will receive more than just accolades. They will benefit from financial support, mentorship opportunities, networking events, and resources to enhance the impact of their projects. The grand prize presentation ceremony will be a distinguished event attended by key stakeholders, sponsors, and the media

Promotion and Awareness

A comprehensive promotional campaign leveraging social media, traditional media, and community outreach programs will be implemented. The aim is to create widespread awareness, encourage university participation, and foster a sense of pride and responsibility in contributing to a sober and healthy society

Read More

Budget

The budget will cover various activities, including awarding five students on each participating campus with start-ups and existing projects, coverage and participation in the selection process, interviewing participants at the international level, organizing the international prize presentation ceremony, and purchasing copies of the book "The Power of Keeping Sober" ("Imbaraga z'Ubishishozi") supplied by the Sober Club and others in the pipeline if they are available. The rewards at all levels will increase as sponsors increase, with Sober Club providing one million rwandan francs for the international awardee.
